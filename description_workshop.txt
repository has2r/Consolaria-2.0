During the development of Terraria 1.3, tons of exclusive mobile & old-gen console features got removed. [b]Consolaria[/b] is a mod that aims to bring this unique content back - fixed, reworked & rebalanced to the level of modern Terraria.

[b]This includes:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Lepus, Turkor & Ocram[/b] - all with brand new AI, Treasure Bags, Master Mode drops & exclusive music themes
[*][b]New & improved vanilla+ loot[/b] - including weapons, armors, mounts & more
[*][b]Removed in-game events[/b] - including Chinese New Year, Valentine's Day, St. Patrick's Day & Oktoberfest
[*][b]Heart Shrine & Jungle Sanctum[/b] underground structures
[*][b]Removed enemies[/b] - including Arch Demon, Vampire Miner, Shadow Mummy & more
[*][b]Console-exclusive vanity items[/b] - including the Horned God set, George's set & more
[*][b]A bunch of console-exclusive pets[/b] - including Guinea Pig, Tiphia, Cupid & more
[*][b][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2669644269]Boss Checklist[/b][/url] & [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2570931073]Fargo's Mutant Mod[/b][/url] support
[*][b]Legendary Holy Hand Grenade![/b]
[/list]

Holiday content is always active by default & can be restricted to the corresponding dates using the config menu.

It is highly recommended to play this mod alongside [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2958674071]Heartbeataria[/url], since both mods complement each other in aim to restore previously unaccessible features making the PC Terraria [b]the most complete experience ever![/b]
