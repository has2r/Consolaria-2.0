[h1]Info[/h1]

Tons of exclusive mobile & old-gen console features were removed with the release of Terraria 1.3. [b]Consolaria[/b] brings this unique content back, reworked and rebalanced for modern Terraria.

[b]This includes:[/b]
[list]
[*][b]Lepus, Turkor & Ocram[/b] - all with brand new AI, Treasure Bags, Master Mode drops & music themes
[*][b]New & improved loot[/b] - including weapons, armors and more
[*][b]In-game events[/b] - Chinese New Year, Valentine's Day, St. Patrick's Day & Oktoberfest
[*][b]Heart Shrine and Jungle Sanctum[/b] structures
[*][b]Arch Wyvern, Vampire Miner, Shadow Mummy[/b] and all the other removed enemies
[*][b]Exclusive vanity items[/b] - including the Horned God set, George's set & many others
[*][b]A variety of ported pets[/b], complete with extra animations
[*][b][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2669644269]Boss Checklist[/url] & [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2570931073]Fargo's Mutant Mod[/url][/b] support
[*][b]Holy Hand Grenade![/b]
[/list]

Holiday content is always active by default and can be restricted to the corresponding dates using the config menu.

Also try [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2958674071]Heartbeataria[/url] for content from Chinese editions of the game: together, the mods make PC Terraria [b]the most complete experience ever![/b]
